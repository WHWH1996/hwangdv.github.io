Hao WANG

Room 3661, HKUST

Clear Water Bay,

Kowloon, Hong Kong SAR

I am a Ph.D candidate in Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), advised by Prof. Kai Chen in intelligent System and networkING Lab (iSING Lab). Before that, I received my B.Eng in Computer Science in Peking University (Outstanding Graduate) in 2019.

My research interests include distributed machine learning system, data center networking, programmable switch and large language models.
